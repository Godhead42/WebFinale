Space Marines

In the grim darkness of the far future, there is only war. Among humanity's greatest champions stand the Space Marines, also known as Adeptus Astartes. They are the elevated sons of the Emperor, having undergone torturous biological transformations and intensive training to become superhuman warriors.

Space Marines are implanted with gene-seed and biological and psychological indoctrination during their transformation. This process, known as the ascension to Astartes, is excruciating yet crucial to create the ultimate protectors of mankind

Each Space Marine belongs to one of the loyalist Chapters that serve the Imperium. The most common are Tactical Marines, equipped with bolt guns and frag grenades for frontline battle.

Space Marine Captains and Chapter Masters lead their brethren in war. Librarians employ sanctioned psychic powers, and Techmarines maintain the Chapter's equipment and vehicles. All are unwavering in their crusade against enemies of the Emperor.

Space Marines fight across the galaxy, whether facing Orks, Chaos, Xenos, Heretics or Daemons. They are humanity's staunchest protectors and most terrifying warriors, known for militant honour, camaraderie and unwavering loyalty to the Imperium.

Each Space Marine Chapter maintains its own traditions and specializes in unique battlefield roles. The Ultramarines emphasize organization and discipline, while the Blood Angels specialize in swift assault tactics.

Chapter forces can number from 100 up to 1,000 battle-brothers. They crew mighty Battle Barges and Strike Cruisers to travel between warzones. On the ground, Space Marines wield relic-armor and an arsenal of weaponry including bolters, flameers, power blades and mighty vehicles.

Despite their small numbers, a single Space Marine is a force multiplier on the battlefield. Relentless and unfazed by pain or hardship, they are living weapons dedicated only to war. During combat, their enhanced biology and resilience makes them near-unstoppable juggernauts who can face waves of enemies with unflagging stamina and skill
